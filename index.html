Sicebi Richman Ndlovu is a man of God, and is continuously seeking the presence of God in his life. He lives by the word “The Lord is my Shepard I shall not lack, He makes me lie down in green pastures, He leads me beside quite water, he refreshes my soul” Psalm 23 vs 1-2 he has a strong conviction to this word.
